## What is Humanta?

**Humanta** is a B2B employee benefits platform that provides curated experience and dinner packages to companies.

**Tagline:** "Where human connection becomes company policy."

**Core Product:** "Humanta Nights" — curated dinner experiences that companies offer to their employees as a benefit.

---

## Target Market

- **Company Size:** 100–500 employees
- **Industries:** Tech, agencies, professional services
- **Location:** Sydney (initial market)
- **Buyer:** HR leaders, People & Culture teams, founders

---

## Business Model

- **Pricing:** $350 AUD per couple per experience
- **Structure:** Quarterly pre-funded pools (avoids multi-year liability issues)
- **Key Insight:** Studied competitor failures (especially Blueboard's financial collapse from multi-year liability accumulation) to design a sustainable financial model
